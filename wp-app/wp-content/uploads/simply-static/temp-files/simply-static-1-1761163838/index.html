Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers.

Everything you need to know about Divertly.

What is Divertly?

Divertly is a URL shortlinking platform that lets you bring your own domain, turning ugly URLs into beautiful ones.

Why should I care what my URLs look like?

Your customers care! Ugly and unprofessional links scare away potential business and erode brand trust. Short, easy-to-digest, and easy-to-remember links that don’t look like viruses make for happy customers.

Can I bring my own domain?

Yes! You can use our default dvt.fyi domain, or you can bring you own.

Can I customize the URL text?

Absolutely! Some link services only let you use random letters and numbers and generate ugly links like li.nk/6ab4df12a4 but Divertly gives you the freedom of choice!

Is it really free?

Yes! Our free tier introduces you to the Divertly platform and lets you create URL’s with our built-in dvt.fyi domain. Upgrade to set up custom domains, routing behavior, and more!

Can I use Terraform or other Infrastructure as Code tools with Divertly?

We are planning on creating a Terraform provider for Divertly — until then, you can use anything that can consume our JSON API.

Do you have an API?

We have a REST-like JSON API you can use to manage your entire account — its the same API used by our admin UI!
